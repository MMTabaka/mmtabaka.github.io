Global Engineering Challenge

A week long team project gathering students from different engineering areas. Our task was to find a solution to frequent power outages and low electrical coverage in Lobitos and Piedritas areas in northern coast of Peru. Our idea was to install small wind turbines near households connected to national power grid and to store energy using car batteries. It would increase the coverage, generate enough energy for a household and with enough amount of batteries also allow residents to sell some of the energy to the national grid.

This project streghen my leadership skills, required me to work in different engineering field than my own as well as improved my presentation and organisation skills.
